FideBot — Fidelidade My Savings

AI assistant to support sales teams with instant, vendor-specific information on investment and saving products.

Organization

Developed for Fidelidade, Portugal’s largest insurance company, with a focus on the My Savings platform — offering financial investment and savings products such as capital-protected plans, ESG-linked options, and retirement savings (PPR Evoluir).

Data Sources

Trained on 83 internal documents using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), including product manuals, FAQs, and vendor guidelines.

Format: Unstructured data 83 docs Financial products Vendor-specific

Analytical Challenges

  • ✓
    Deliver accurate, real-time product details for My Savings and PPR Evoluir.
  • ✓
    Support sales teams in explaining complex investment and savings options to clients with different risk profiles.
  • ✓
    Balance accuracy, speed, and cost-effectiveness in deployment.
  • ✓
    Promote financial literacy by simplifying product explanations for both agents and customers.

Methods

Implemented RAG pipelines powered by Azure OpenAI embeddings and GPT models, with data stored in Astra DB and flows orchestrated in Langflow.

Fine-tuned to prioritize accuracy, financial reliability, and usability for sales representatives.
